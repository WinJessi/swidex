At SGR we provide services such as:

01

Daily Savings

Creates different saving plans for customers and helps them to reach a certain goal(amount) set by themselves. This is also flexible as the users can opt out if it’s not a fixed savings.

02

Loans

We provide loans to small and medium scale businesses to meet various business requirements. These loans can be used for a variety of purposes that help in growing the business.

03

Fixed savings

Users can make fixed deposits and set a withdrawing time. Until the time is ripe for withdrawal, users cannot have access to the money but can add to it through custom credit or auto debit.

04

Travel Funds

Proof of Funds (POF) is a letter or documentation that proves an individual traveling, institution or corporation has sufficient funds to complete a transaction.
